Medication Inventory Dashboard



• Context

The Hemodynamics Department at Hapvida Hospital faced challenges in monitoring the number of procedures performed across Brazil, comparing performance between different units, and analyzing more granular details such as regional distribution, patient age, and gender. This analysis was developed to provide comprehensive insights into procedure trends, enabling data-driven decisions across the network.

• Insights

  • Regional Performance: Identified regions with higher or lower procedure volumes, highlighting operational gaps.
  • Demographic Analysis: Analyzed trends by age and gender, helping tailor patient care strategies.
  • Procedure Trends Over Time: Detected seasonal variations and peak periods, optimizing scheduling and resource allocation.
  • Unit Comparison: Benchmarked hospital units, identifying top-performing centers and areas needing improvement.
  • Efficiency Indicators: Highlighted potential inefficiencies and opportunities for process optimization.

These insights allow hospital management to improve resource allocation, streamline procedures, and enhance patient outcomes.

• Technical Stack

  • Tools: Power BI, Python (Pandas), Excel
  • Data Source: Excel Spreadsheets
  • Data Modeling: Star Schema with Fact and Dimension Tables
