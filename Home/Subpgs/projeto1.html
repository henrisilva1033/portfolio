Medication Inventory Dashboard



• Context

The NotreLabs department at Hapvida Hospital faced challenges in monitoring its medication inventory, predicting shortages of critical drugs, and tracking expiration dates.This analysis was developed to centralize key indicators of consumption, item validity, and withdrawal status, providing full visibility across all hospital units.

• Insights

The dashboard presents batch-level consumption data, highlights the most frequently used categories, and tracks medications approaching expiration. By enabling proactive decision-making, it helped reduce waste and prevent shortages of essential supplies. As a result, the solution significantly improved inventory management and medication control across laboratories.

• Technical Stack

  • Tools: Power BI, Python (Pandas), Excel
  • Data Source: CSV Exports
  • Data Modeling: Star Schema with Fact and Dimension Tables
