Supermarket Sales Analysis



• Context

Before this project, the finance department relied on manual extraction of financial data from multiple systems into Excel spreadsheets — a process that took around 8 hours and required significant manual adjustments. The financial BI report was updated only once per month, limiting timely decision-making.

The project was designed to automate the full financial reporting workflow, providing continuous, accurate, and up-to-date insights into company performance.

• Insights

Power BI dashboards were connected directly to the warehouse to visualize metrics such as Revenue, Gross Margin, Operating Expenses, and Net Income in real time. This eliminated manual reporting and empowered finance leaders with instant, data-driven insights to support strategic decisions.

The project demonstrates strong expertise in data engineering, process automation, and financial analytics, translating directly into measurable business value.

• Technical Stack

  • Tools: Power BI, Python (Pandas), FABRIC: DFGen2 | DW (SQL Server) | PowerBi ,
  • Data Source: ERP: PostgreSQL
  • Data Modeling:Medallion architecture and Star Schema with Fact and Dimension Tables
